How to Use The App

  1. First input the number of courses you want to calculate for. *Maximum of 50 courses at once
  2. Select your Institution Grading System * University, College of Education or Polytechnic
  3. Wait for about 2 seconds ( Depending on your device speed) for the APP to load the input page
  4. Start inputing your SCORE and the unit of the COURSE one after the other
  5. Tap the CALCULATE button after completing the field or CLEAR if you want to reset the form
  6. Make Sure All the fields are completed
